About Me

Here's to all I love doing.

Mostly, I rock climb. Preferably outside, but most often inside a gym on plastic holds. I love the movement of climbing. Climbing can force you to trust, balance, wrestle, or make a ninja-like coordination move. If you watch any baby or todler, you'll remember that we humans were born to climb.

Though climbing is easily my favorite sport, it's nearly impossinble to only like ONE extreme sport. Either they scare you or you want to do them all. I love the thrill of mountain biking, the height and balance on highline slacklining, pumping through deep powder on a snowboard, the danger of ice climbing, and anything else you can do in the mountains.

Here is a short list of my favorite adventures in the mountains:

  • Climbing the Totem Pole in Arizona
  • Completing Imlay Canyon in 17 hours
  • Running a marathon with no training in 4.5 hours
  • Highlining over Rock Canyon
  • Solo summit attempt of Mt Timpanogos in February
